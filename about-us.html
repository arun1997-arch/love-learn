About Us

Love & Learn Preschool , was founded to achieve a goal to spread knowledge in this environment where there is a belief that all children should be molded in their capacity and become a independent learner before they being their competition with the core education of formal schools. Love & Learn Preschool believes that childhood of every tiny one is important and precises,where the childhood is to be enjoyed and cherished over the period of time. The first five years of every child indelicate,where incredible learning vital mental and cognitive growth occurs. In these years of life the child has an outstanding ability to flourish and excel in things at a amazing pace. Our programs are tailored to brighten every child’s social, physical, and aesthetics skills. We develop age appropriate educational activity for child’s all round development.

Our Philosophy

Love & Learn Preschool believes in philosophy that enables every child potential through his/her individual learning and time. Based Plans. We recognize the different learning styles,abilities and interests of each child. A strong team of mentors and support staff help create a HAPPY environment where children are eager to learn.

Courses Offered by Love & Learn Preschool

Our playgroup program is designed for toddlers to explore and learn through interactive activities, storytelling, music, and creative play.

Our Lower Kindergarten (LKG) program introduces children to structured learning with language development, numeracy skills, and creative expression.

Our Upper Kindergarten (UKG) curriculum builds upon early education with a focus on language fluency, early mathematics, and creative problem-solving.

Our after-school care includes arts, crafts, music, dance, and storytelling, keeping children engaged beyond school hours.

We provide tuition services for LKG to 8th standard, covering Mathematics, Science, and English with interactive learning techniques.

Our daycare provides a nurturing environment with trained caregivers, engaging activities, and nutritious meals.
